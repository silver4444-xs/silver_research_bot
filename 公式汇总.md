## 1. Age-of-Task-Aware AAV

### 1.1 控制模型

**AAV 在时隙 $t$ 的速度:**
$$v_t = \frac{\|U(t + 1) - U(t)\|_2}{\tau}$$

**第 $i$ 个时隙内的飞行距离:**
$$l_{i,i+1} = \|U(i + 1) - U(i)\|_2$$

**飞行速度约束:**
$$l_{t,t+1} \le v_{\max} \cdot \tau, \quad 1 \le t \le T - 1$$

### 1.2 通信模型

**信息传输速率:**
$$R_k(t) = a_k(t) \cdot B \cdot \log_2 \left( 1 + \frac{p_k^{tr}(t) \cdot h_k(t)}{\sigma^2} \right)$$
其中 $h_k(t) = h_0 \cdot (d_k^2(t) + H^2)^{-1}$

**调度指示变量:**
$$a_k(t) = \begin{cases} 1, & \text{节点在时隙 t 决定进行任务卸载}, \\ 0, & \text{该节点等待在未来的时隙中进行传输}. \end{cases}$$

**平均发射功率约束:**
$$\lim_{T \to \infty} \frac{1}{T} \sum_{t=1}^{T} (a_k(t) \cdot p_k^{tr}(t)) \le \bar{p}$$

**距离平方:**
$$d_k^2(t) = \|U(t) - G_k\|_2^2$$

### 1.3 计算模型

**覆盖半径:**
$$C = H \cdot \tan(\theta)$$

**覆盖约束:**
$$a_k(t) \cdot d_k^2(t) \le C^2$$

**子信道约束:**
$$\sum_{k=1}^{K} a_k(t) \le Z, \quad Z = \lfloor W/B \rfloor$$

**卸载总时延:**
$$T_k^{off}(t) = T_k^{tr}(t) + T_k^{comp}(t)$$

**传输时延:**
$$T_k^{tr}(t) = \frac{D_k(t)}{R_k(t)}$$

**计算资源约束:**
$$0 \le f_k^{uav}(t) \le F^{\max},\quad \sum_{k=1}^{K} (a_k(t) \cdot f_k^{uav}(t)) \le F^{\max}$$

**计算时延:**
$$T_k^{comp}(t) = \frac{D_k(t) \cdot F_k(t)}{a_k(t) \cdot f_k^{uav}(t)}$$

**任务完成标志:**
$$m_k(t) = \begin{cases} 1, & \text{if } T_k^{off}(t) \le \tau \\ 0, & \text{otherwise.} \end{cases}$$

**队列演化:**
$$Q_k(t + 1) = (Q_k(t) + g_k(t)D_k(t) - a_k(t)m_k(t)D_k(t))^+$$

### 1.4 能耗模型

**总能耗:**
$$E(t) = \sum_{k=1}^{K} a_k(t)E_k^{comp}(t) + E^{fly}(t)$$

**计算能耗:**
$$E_k^{comp}(t) = c \cdot (f_k^{uav}(t))^\alpha \cdot T_k^{comp}(t)$$

**推进功率:**
$$P_{mov}(v_t) = P_0 \cdot \left( 1 + \frac{3v_t^2}{U_{tip}^2} \right) + P_1 \cdot \left( \left( 1 + \frac{v_t^4}{4v_0^4} \right)^{\frac{1}{2}} - \frac{v_t^2}{2v_0^2} \right)^{1/2} + \frac{1}{2} d_0 \rho s_0 \delta v_t^3$$

**飞行能耗:**
$$E^{fly}(t) = P_{mov}(v_t) \cdot \tau$$

**总能耗约束:**
$$E_{uav} = \sum_{t=1}^{T} \left( \sum_{k=1}^{K} a_k(t)E_k^{comp}(t) + E^{fly}(t) \right) \le E_{\max}$$

### 1.5 AoT (Age of Task) 模型

**AoT 演化:**
$$A_k(t + 1) = \begin{cases} t + 1 - \mathcal{G}_k(t), & a_k(t) \cdot m_k(t) = 1 \\ A_k(t) + 1, & \text{otherwise} \end{cases}$$

**时均 AoT:**
$$\bar{A}_k = \frac{1}{T} \sum_{t=1}^{T} A_k(t)$$

**加权 AoT:**
$$\bar{A} = \sum_{k=1}^{K} w_k \left( \frac{1}{T} \sum_{t=1}^{T} A_k(t) \right)$$

### 1.6 问题表述 (P1)

$$\min_{U(t), a_k(t), f_k^{uav}(t), p_k^{tr}(t)} \sum_{k=1}^{K} w_k \left( \frac{1}{T} \sum_{t=1}^{T} A_k(t) \right)$$

$$\begin{aligned} \mathbf{P1}: \min & \sum_{k=1}^{K} w_k \left( \frac{1}{T} \sum_{t=1}^{T} A_k(t) \right) \\ \text{s.t.} \quad & l_{t,t+1} \leq v_{\max} \cdot \tau, \; 1 \leq t \leq T - 1 & (27\text{a}) \\ & a_k(t) \in \{0, 1\} \quad \forall k, t & (27\text{b}) \\ & \sum_{k=1}^{K} a_k(t) \leq Z \quad \forall t & (27\text{c}) \\ & \sum_{k=1}^{K} a_k(t)f_k^{uav}(t) \leq F^{\max} \quad \forall t & (27\text{d}) \\ & T_k^{off}(t) = T_k^{tr}(t) + T_k^{comp}(t) \quad \forall k, t & (27\text{e}) \\ & m_k(t) = \begin{cases} 1, \text{ if } T_k^{off}(t) \leq \tau \\ \\ 0, \text{ otherwise} \end{cases} \quad \forall k & (27\text{f}) \\ & \lim_{T \to \infty} \frac{\sum_{t=1}^{T} g_k(t)D_k(t)}{T} \leq \lim_{T \to \infty} \frac{\sum_{t=1}^{T} a_k(t)m_k(t)D_k(t)}{T} \quad \forall k & (27\text{g}) \\ & E_{uav} \leq E_{\max} & (27\text{h}) \\ & A_k(t + 1) = \begin{cases} t + 1 - \mathcal{G}_k(t), & a_k(t) \cdot m_k(t) = 1 \\ \\ A_k(t) + 1, & \text{otherwise} \end{cases} \quad \forall k & (27\text{i}) \\ & 0 \leq f_k^{uav}(t) \leq F^{\max} \quad \forall t, k & (27\text{j}) \\ & a_k(t) \cdot d_k^2(t) \leq C^2 \quad \forall k, t & (27\text{k}) \\ & 0 \leq p_k^{tr}(t) \leq P_{\max} \quad \forall k, t & (27\text{l}) \\ & \lim_{T \to \infty} \frac{1}{T} \sum_{t=1}^{T} a_k(t)p_k^{tr}(t) \leq \bar{p} \quad \forall k & (27\text{m}) \end{aligned}$$

## 2. Computation offloading optimization for UAV-assisted mobile edge

> **注意**: 本文公式均为 PNG 图片格式，无法提取 LaTeX 源码。以下记录公式的语义描述。

### 2.1 通信模型
- 视距链路信道增益: $g = a_0 / d_k^2$（参考距离1m处增益/距离平方）
- 传输速率: $R = B \log_2(1 + P_{up} \cdot g / (\sigma^2 + P_{NLOS}))$，考虑遮挡指示变量

### 2.2 计算模型
- 本地执行时延: $T_{loc} = D_k(i) \cdot s / f_{UE}$
- 飞行能耗: $E_{fly}$ 与无人机有效载荷M和飞行时间t相关
- 传输时延: $T_{trans} = (1-R_k) \cdot D_k / R_k(i)$
- MEC计算时延: $T_{comp} = (1-R_k) \cdot D_k \cdot s / f_{UAV}$
- MEC服务器功耗: $P = \kappa \cdot f_{UAV}^3$

### 2.3 优化问题 (P1)
$$\min \max_{k} T_k$$，约束包括: 用户调度 (9b-c)、卸载比 (9d)、区域限制 (9e-f)、遮挡 (9g)、电池容量 (9h)、任务完成 (9i)

### 2.4 DDPG 算法
- 状态: $s_i = \{E_{battery}(i), q_i, p_k, D_{remain}(i), D_k(i), \text{遮挡指示}\}$
- 动作: $a_i = \{k_0, \theta(i), v(i), R_k(i)\}$，包含离散化处理
- 奖励: 最小化问题(9)中定义的处理延迟的最大奖励

## 3. Computation-Efficient Aerial-Marine Integrated Networks

### 3.1 通信模型

**服务器选择决策:**
$$s_i^m = \begin{cases} 1, & \text{UAV i 在时隙 t 中选择服务器 m 处理任务} \\ 0, & \text{otherwise} \end{cases}$$

**大尺度路径损耗 (UAV-MASS):**
$$L_i^M|_{\text{dB}} = L_0 + 10\lambda \log_{10} \left( \frac{d_i^m}{d_0} \right) + X_\sigma + \varrho F$$

**小尺度莱斯衰落:**
$$\tilde{u}_i^m = \sqrt{\frac{K_R}{1+K_R}} + \sqrt{\frac{1}{1+K_R}} g_i^m, \quad g_i^m \sim \mathcal{CN}(0, 1)$$

**信道系数:**
$$h_i^m = (L_i^M)^{-1/2} \tilde{u}_i^m$$

**信道增益:**
$$H_i^m = G_U G_M |h_i^m|^2$$

**UAV-HAPS 路径损耗:**
$$L_i^H = 20 \log_{10} \left( \frac{4\pi f_i d_i^H}{c} \right) + \gamma_i^{\text{LoS}} \delta_i^{\text{LoS}} + (1 - \gamma_i^{\text{LoS}}) \delta_i^{\text{NLoS}}$$

**LoS 概率:**
$$\gamma_i^{\text{LoS}} = \frac{1}{1 + \chi_1 \exp[-\chi_2(\phi_i - \chi_1)]}, \quad \phi_i = \tan^{-1} \left( \frac{z_H - H_0}{\sqrt{(x_H - x_i)^2 + (y_H - y_i)^2}} \right)$$

**HAPS 信道增益:**
$$H_i^m = 10^{-L_i^H/10}, \quad m = 0$$

**香农容量:**
$$R_i^m = s_i^m W_i^m \log_2 \left( 1 + \frac{p_i H_i^m}{\sigma^2} \right)$$

### 3.2 计算模型

**本地计算时延:**
$$t_i^{\text{loc}} = \frac{a_i D \alpha}{\rho_i}$$

**本地计算能耗:**
$$E_i^{\text{loc}} = \epsilon_i (\rho_i)^2 a_i D \alpha$$

**边缘计算总时延:**
$$t_i^{\text{edge}} = t_i^{\text{off}} + t_i^{\text{com}}$$

**卸载传输时延:**
$$t_i^{\text{off}} = \frac{a_i D}{R_i^m}$$

**卸载传输能耗:**
$$E_i^{\text{off}} = p_i t_i^{\text{off}} = \frac{p_i a_i D}{R_i^m}$$

**边缘计算执行时间:**
$$t_i^{\text{com}} = \frac{a_i D \alpha}{\beta_i^m F_m}$$

**边缘计算能耗:**
$$E_i^{\text{com}} = \epsilon_m (\beta_i^m F_m)^2 a_i D \alpha$$

**边缘计算总能耗:**
$$E_i^{\text{edge}} = E_i^{\text{off}} + E_i^{\text{com}} = \frac{p_i a_i D}{R_i^m} + \epsilon_m (\beta_i^m F_m)^2 a_i D \alpha$$

### 3.3 计算开销 (CO)

**任务完成时延:**
$$T_i = s_i^m t_i^{\text{edge}} + (1 - s_i^m) t_i^{\text{loc}}$$

**能耗:**
$$E_i = s_i^m E_i^{\text{edge}} + (1 - s_i^m) E_i^{\text{loc}}$$

**计算开销 (CO):**
$$G_i = \mu_i^t T_i + \mu_i^e E_i, \quad \mu_i^t + \mu_i^e = 1$$

### 3.4 问题表述 (P1)

$$\min_{\mathbf{Z}=\{\mathbf{s}, \mathbf{p}, \mathbf{\beta}, \mathbf{\rho}\}} \mathcal{G} = \sum_{i \in \mathcal{I}} G_i$$

$$\begin{aligned} \mathbf{(P1):} \min_{\mathbf{Z}} \quad & \mathcal{G} \\ \text{s.t.} \quad & 0 \leq a_i \leq a^{\max}, \quad \forall i \in \mathcal{I}, & (20\text{a}) \\ & s_i^m \in \{0, 1\}, \quad \forall i \in \mathcal{I}, \; \forall m \in \mathcal{M}, & (20\text{b}) \\ & \sum_{m=0}^{M} s_i^m \leq 1, \quad \forall i \in \mathcal{I}, & (20\text{c}) \\ & 0 \leq p_i \leq p_i^{\max}, \quad \forall i \in \mathcal{I}, & (20\text{d}) \\ & 0 \leq \rho_i \leq \rho_i^{\max}, \quad \forall i \in \mathcal{I}, & (20\text{e}) \\ & 0 \leq \beta_i^m \leq 1, \quad \forall i \in \mathcal{I}, \quad \forall m \in \mathcal{M}, & (20\text{f}) \\ & \sum_{i \in \Lambda_m} \beta_i^m \leq 1, \quad \forall m \in \mathcal{M}, & (20\text{g}) \\ & t_i^{loc} \leq T_i^{th}, \quad \forall i \in \mathcal{I}, & (20\text{h}) \\ & t_i^{edge} \leq T_i^{th}, \quad \forall i \in \mathcal{I}. & (20\text{i}) \end{aligned}$$

## 4. Cooperative data collection with multiple UAVs for information freshness

### 4.1 控制模型

**速度极坐标表示:**
$$\boldsymbol{v}_m(t) = (v_m^s(t), \varphi_m(t))$$

**旋翼推力:**
$$T_m^{\mathrm{h}}(t) = \frac{1}{n_r} \left[ \left( W a_{c, m}(t) + \frac{1}{2} \rho (v_m^{\mathrm{s}}(t))^2 S_{F P} \right)^2 + (W g)^2 \right]^{\frac{1}{2}}$$

### 4.2 能量模型

**推进能耗 (复杂公式):**
$$\begin{aligned} E_m(t) = & \tau_0 n_r \left[ \frac{\sigma}{8} \left( \frac{T_m^{\mathrm{h}}(t)}{c_T \rho A} + 3 (v_m^{\mathrm{s}}(t))^2 \right) \sqrt{\frac{T_m^{\mathrm{h}}(t) \rho c_s^2 A}{c_T}} \right. \\ & + \frac{1}{2} d_0 \rho c_s A (v_m^{\mathrm{s}}(t))^3 + (1 + c_f) T_m^{\mathrm{h}}(t) \\ & \times \left. \left( \sqrt{\frac{(T_m^{\mathrm{h}}(t))^2}{4 \rho^2 A^2} + \frac{(v_m^{\mathrm{s}}(t))^4}{4}} - \frac{(v_m^{\mathrm{s}}(t))^2}{2} \right)^{\frac{1}{2}} \right] \end{aligned}$$

**SN 传输能耗:**
$$E_n^{\mathrm{c}} = P_c \tau_0$$

**SN 电池动态:**
$$E_n^{\mathrm{sn}}(t + 1) = \begin{cases} \min\{E_n^{\mathrm{sn}}(t) + \kappa_n(t) E_n^{\mathrm{har}} - E_n^{\mathrm{c}}, E_{\max}^{\mathrm{sn}}\}, & \text{if } \zeta_n(t) = 1 \\ \min\{E_n^{\mathrm{sn}}(t) + \kappa_n(t) E_n^{\mathrm{har}}, E_{\max}^{\mathrm{sn}}\}, & \text{otherwise} \end{cases}$$

### 4.3 通信模型

**LoS 概率:**
$$p_{n,m}^{\text{LoS}}(t) = \frac{1}{1 + \beta_0 \exp \left( -\beta_1 \left( \frac{180}{\pi} \arcsin \left( \frac{z}{d_{n,m}(t)} \right) - \beta_0 \right) \right)}$$

**路径损耗:**
$$PL_{n,m}(t) = \begin{cases} (\frac{4\pi f_c d_{n,m}(t)}{c})^\varsigma \eta_{\text{LoS}}, & \text{w.p. } p_{n,m}^{\text{LoS}}(t) \\ (\frac{4\pi f_c d_{n,m}(t)}{c})^\varsigma \eta_{\text{NLoS}}, & \text{otherwise} \end{cases}$$

**SINR:**
$$\xi_{n,m}(t) = \frac{P_c G_s G_u \gamma_{n,m}(t)}{\sigma^2 + I_{n,m}(t)}$$

其中 $I_{n,m}(t) = \sum_{n' \in \mathcal{N}_{-m}(t)/n} P_c \gamma_{n',m}(t)$

### 4.4 感知模型

**覆盖半径:**
$$R_U = \sqrt{d^2 - z^2}, \quad d = \frac{c}{4\pi f_c} \left( \frac{P_c G_s G_u}{\xi_{\text{th}} \sigma^2 \eta_{\text{NLoS}}} \right)^{1/\varsigma}$$

### 4.5 AoI 模型

**AoI 动态:**
$$\delta_n(t + 1) = \begin{cases} 1, & \text{if } \zeta_n(t) = 1 \text{ and } \xi_{n,m}(t) \geq \xi_{\text{th}} \\ \min(\delta_n(t) + 1, \delta_{\max}), & \text{otherwise} \end{cases}$$

### 4.6 问题表述 (P1)

$$\min_{\boldsymbol{V}, \boldsymbol{B}} \frac{1}{T} \mathbb{E} \left[ \sum_{t=1}^T \sum_{n=1}^N \delta_n(t) \right]$$

$$\begin{aligned} \text{P1: } \min_{\boldsymbol{V}, \boldsymbol{B}} \quad & \frac{1}{T} \mathbb{E} \left[ \sum_{t=1}^T \sum_{n=1}^N \delta_n(t) \right] \\ \text{s.t.} \quad & d_{m,m'}(t) \geq d_{\text{safe}}, \forall m, m' \in \mathcal{M}, m \neq m', & (8\text{b}) \\ & 0 \leq v_m^s(t) \leq v_{\max}^s, \forall m \in \mathcal{M}, & (8\text{c}) \\ & 0 \leq \varphi_m(t) \leq 2\pi, \forall m \in \mathcal{M}, & (8\text{d}) \\ & |\triangle\varphi_m(t)| \leq \triangle\varphi_{\max}, \forall m \in \mathcal{M}, t \geq 2, & (8\text{e}) \\ & \zeta_n(t) \leq \mathbf{1}(E_n(t) \geq E_n^c, \bar{d}_{n,m}(t) \leq R_{\text{U}}), \forall n \in \mathcal{N}, \exists m \in \mathcal{M}, & (8\text{f}) \\ & \sum_{t=1}^T E_m(t) \leq E_{\max}, \forall m \in \mathcal{M}, & (8\text{g}) \\ & \boldsymbol{u}_m(1) = \boldsymbol{u}_m^{\text{start}}, \boldsymbol{u}_m(T) = \boldsymbol{u}_m^{\text{stop}}, \forall m \in \mathcal{M}. & (8\text{h}) \end{aligned}$$

### 4.7 DQN 模型

**全局状态:**
$$\boldsymbol{s}(t) = (\boldsymbol{u}(t), \boldsymbol{\delta}(t), \boldsymbol{V}(t), \boldsymbol{\varphi}(t-1), \boldsymbol{E}^{\text{sn}}(t), \boldsymbol{\phi}(t), \boldsymbol{\psi}(t))$$

**局部观测:**
$$\boldsymbol{o}_m(t) = (\boldsymbol{u}_m(t), \hat{\boldsymbol{\delta}}_m(t), v_m^s(t), \varphi_m(t-1), \hat{\boldsymbol{E}}_m^{\text{sn}}(t), \phi_m(t), \psi_m(t))$$

**动作:**
$$\boldsymbol{a}_m(t) = (v_m^s(t+1), \varphi_m(t), b_m(t))$$

**动作空间:**
$$\mathcal{A} = \mathcal{V}^s \times \Phi \times \mathcal{B}$$

**可用移动集合:**
$$\mathcal{V}_m'(t) \triangleq \{v_m^s(t+1), \varphi_m(t) \mid v_m^s(t+1) \in \mathcal{V}^s, \varphi_m(t) \in \Phi, (\varphi_m(t-1) - \triangle\varphi_{\max}) \bmod 2\pi \leq \varphi_m(t) \leq (\varphi_m(t-1) + \triangle\varphi_{\max}) \bmod 2\pi\}$$

**可用调度集合:**
$$\mathcal{B}_m'(t) = \{n \mid n \in \mathcal{B}, \bar{d}_{n,m}(t) \leq R_{\text{U}}, E_n(t) \geq E_n^c\}$$

**代价:**
$$c(t) = \begin{cases} \sum_{n=1}^N \delta_n(t) + k_1, & \text{if } d_{m,m'}(t) < d_{\text{safe}} \\ \sum_{n=1}^N \delta_n(t), & \text{otherwise} \end{cases}$$

**位置更新:**
$$\boldsymbol{u}_m(t+1) = \boldsymbol{u}_m(t) + \left( \frac{v_m^s(t) + v_m^s(t+1)}{2} \cos \varphi_m(t) \tau_0, \frac{v_m^s(t) + v_m^s(t+1)}{2} \sin \varphi_m(t) \tau_0 \right)$$

**时间差更新:**
$$\phi_m(t+1) = \phi_m(t) - 1 + T_m^{\text{req}}(t) - T_m^{\text{req}}(t+1)$$

**能量差更新:**
$$\psi_m(t+1) = \psi_m(t) - E_m(t) + E_m^{\text{req}}(t) - E_m^{\text{req}}(t+1)$$

## 5. Deep Reinforcement Learning for Minimizing...

### 5.1 通信模型

**信道功率增益:**
$$g_{u,m}(n) = \beta_0 d_{u,m}^{-2} = \frac{\beta_0}{h^2 + \|L_u(n) - L_m\|^2}$$

### 5.2 控制模型

**位置动态:**
$$L_u(n+1) = \begin{cases} L_u(n) + (0, y_s), & \text{if } v(n) = N \\ L_u(n) - (0, y_s), & \text{if } v(n) = S \\ L_u(n) + (x_s, 0), & \text{if } v(n) = E \\ L_u(n) - (x_s, 0), & \text{if } v(n) = W \\ L_u(n), & \text{otherwise} \end{cases}$$

### 5.3 AoI 模型

**AoI 动态:**
$$A_m(n+1) = \begin{cases} 1, & \text{if } w(n) = m \\ \min \{A_{\max, m}, A_m(n) + 1\}, & \text{otherwise} \end{cases}$$

### 5.4 能量模型

**传输所需能量量子数:**
$$e_m^{\text{T}}(n) = \lceil \frac{e_{\max, m}}{E_{\max, m}} E_m^{\text{T}}(n) \rceil$$

### 8.1 控制模型

$$\boldsymbol{v}_j(t + 1) = \boldsymbol{v}_j(t) + \boldsymbol{a}_j(t)\delta_t$$

$$\sum_{j=1}^{J} x_{ij}(t) = 1,\quad \sum_{i=1}^{I} x_{ij}(t) \le N_A$$

$$h_{ij}(t) = \frac{G_0 G_1 \beta_0}{\|\boldsymbol{l}_i(t) - \boldsymbol{l}_j(t)\|_2^2}$$

**ABS-MBS 信道增益:**
$$h_{jg}(t) = \frac{G_0 G_2 \beta_0}{\|\boldsymbol{l}_j(t) - \boldsymbol{l}_g(t)\|_2^2}$$

**传输速率:**
$$r_{ij}(t) = x_{ij}(t)B \log_2 \left( 1 + \frac{p \cdot h_{ij}(t)}{N_0} \right)$$

$$Q_j^\text{O}(t + 1) = \max \{ Q_j^\text{O}(t) + \gamma_j(t)C_j(t) - \sum_{g=1}^{G} r_{jg}(t)\delta_t, 0 \}$$

其中 $Z_j(t) = \min ( \sum_{g=1}^{G} r_{jg}(t)\delta_t, Q_j^\text{O}(t) + \gamma_j(t)C_j(t) )$

**排队时延 (Little定律):**
$$T_j^\text{L}(t) = \frac{Q_j^\text{L}(t)}{D_j^\text{L}(t)},\quad T_j^\text{O}(t) = \frac{Q_j^\text{O}(t)}{\sum_{g=1}^{G} r_{jg}(t)},\quad T_j^\text{S}(t) = \frac{Q_j^\text{S}(t)}{D_j^\text{S}(t)}$$

### 8.4 能耗模型

**推进功率:**
$$P(\boldsymbol{v}_j(t)) = c_1 \|\boldsymbol{v}_j(t)\|^3 + \frac{c_2}{\|\boldsymbol{v}_j(t)\|} \left( 1 + \frac{\|\boldsymbol{a}_j(t)\|^2}{\alpha^2} \right)$$

### 8.5 问题表述 (P1)

$$\min_{\boldsymbol{X}, \boldsymbol{D}, \boldsymbol{\gamma}} \frac{1}{T} \sum_{t=1}^{T} \sum_{j=1}^{J} \left( T_j^{\text{L}}(t) + T_j^{\text{O}}(t) + T_j^{\text{S}}(t) \right)$$

BS部分: 含缓存/无缓存两种情况的处理+传输时延

**协作处理UAV本地计算能耗:**

**协作处理UAV传输能耗:**
$$E_{j \to i}^{co, trs}(t) = P_{j}^{trs} \frac{\vartheta_{i,j}(t) D_{i}(t)}{R_{j}^{BS}(t)}$$

**协作总能耗:**
$$E_{j \to i}^{co}(t) = E_{j \to i}^{co, pro}(t) + E_{j \to i}^{co, trs}(t)$$

**PD任务处理时延:**
$$T_{i,j}(t) = (1 - \kappa_{i,j}(t))T_{i,j}^{u}(t) + \kappa_{i,j}(t)T_{i,j}^{co}(t)$$

**PD总时延:**
$$T_{i}(t) = \sum_{j=1}^{J} \alpha_{i,j}(t)[(1 - \kappa_{i,j}(t))T_{i,j}^{u}(t) + \kappa_{i,j}(t)T_{i,j}^{co}(t)]$$

**UAV处理任务能耗:**
$$E_{j \to i}^{task}(t) = (1 - \kappa_{i,j}(t))E_{j \to i}^{pure}(t) + \kappa_{i,j}(t)E_{j \to i}^{co}(t)$$

**UAV总任务处理能耗:**
$$E_{j}^{task}(t) = \sum_{i \in \mathcal{I}_j} E_{j \to i}^{task}(t)$$

**飞行能耗:**
$$E_j^{fly}(t) = P_j^{fly} \frac{d_j^{fly}(t)}{V_j(t)}$$

**剩余电量:**
$$E_j^{rm}(t) = E_j^{rm}(t - 1) - E_j^{task}(t) - E_j^{fly}(t)$$

### 12.6 目标函数

**经济成本:**
$$Cost_{j \to i}(t) = \rho^{BS} \vartheta_{i,j}(t)D_i(t),\quad Cost_{j}(t) = \sum_{i \in \mathcal{I}_j} \kappa_{i,j}(t)\rho^{BS} \vartheta_{i,j}(t)D_i(t)$$

**UAV生命:**
$$T_j^{u,life}(t) = (t - 1) \cdot \mathbb{I}(\{E_j^{rm}(t) < E^{th}\} \& \{E_j^{rm}(t - 1) > E^{th}\})$$

**网络生命:**
$$T^{life} = \min \{T_j^{u,life}(t)\}$$

**目标函数:**
$$Obj = \eta_1 \varsigma_1 T^{life} - \eta_2 \varsigma_2 \sum_{t \in \mathcal{T}^{life}} \sum_{j \in \mathcal{J}} Cost_j(t)$$

### 12.7 问题表述 (P1)

$$\max_{\boldsymbol{\Delta}(t), \mathbf{V}(t), \boldsymbol{\alpha}(t), \boldsymbol{\omega}(t), \boldsymbol{\kappa}(t), \boldsymbol{\vartheta}(t)} Obj$$

$$\begin{aligned} (\mathcal{P}_{\mathbf{1}}) : \max_{\substack{\boldsymbol{\Delta}(t), \mathbf{V}(t), \boldsymbol{\alpha}(t), \\ \boldsymbol{\omega}(t), \boldsymbol{\kappa}(t), \boldsymbol{\vartheta}(t)}} & Obj \\ \text{s.t. } & (\text{C1}) : \Delta x_j(t), \Delta y_j(t), \Delta z_j(t) \in [\Delta_{min}, \Delta_{max}], j \in \mathcal{J}, \\ & (\text{C2}) : V_j(t) \geq \frac{d_j^{fly}(t)}{\Delta t}, j \in \mathcal{J}, \\ & (\text{C3}) : 0 \leq X_j(t), Y_j(t) \leq L, j \in \mathcal{J}, \\ & (\text{C4}) : H^{min} \leq H_j(t) \leq H^{max}, j \in \mathcal{J}, \\ & (\text{C5}) : d_{j,j'}(t) \geq d^{safe}, j \in \mathcal{J}, j' \in \mathcal{J}, \\ & (\text{C6}) : \alpha_{i,j}(t) \in \{0, 1\}, i \in \mathcal{I}, j \in \mathcal{J}, \\ & (\text{C7}) : \sum_{j \in \mathcal{J}} \alpha_{i,j}(t) = 1, i \in \mathcal{I}, \\ & (\text{C8}) : \alpha_{i,j}(t) d_{i,j}(t) \leq d^{thre}, i \in \mathcal{I}, j \in \mathcal{J}, \\ & (\text{C9}) : \omega_{i,j}(t) \in \{0, 1\}, i \in \mathcal{I}, j \in \mathcal{J}, \\ & (\text{C10}) : \sum_{i \in \mathcal{I}_j} \omega_{i,j}(t) D_i(t) \leq \Pi_j, j \in \mathcal{J}, \\ & (\text{C11}) : \kappa_{i,j}(t) \in \{0, 1\}, i \in \mathcal{I}, j \in \mathcal{J}, \\ & (\text{C12}) : 0 \leq \vartheta_{i,j}(t) \leq 1, i \in \mathcal{I}, j \in \mathcal{J}, \\ & (\text{C13}) : T_i(t) \leq \Delta t, i \in \mathcal{I} \end{aligned}$$

### 12.8 MADDPG 模型

**局部观测:**
$$z_j(t) \triangleq \{ \mathbf{u}_j(t - 1), \mathbf{D}(t), \boldsymbol{\lambda}(t), \tilde{\mathbf{f}}(t), E_j^{rm}(t - 1), T_j^{u,life}(t - 1) \}$$

**全局状态:**
$$\mathbf{s}(t) \triangleq \{ z_1(t), z_2(t), \dots, z_J(t) \}$$

**动作:**
$$\mathbf{a}_j(t) \triangleq \{ \Delta_j(t), V_j(t), \boldsymbol{\alpha}(t), \boldsymbol{\omega}_j(t), \boldsymbol{\kappa}_j(t), \boldsymbol{\vartheta}_j(t) \}$$

**全局动作:**
$$\mathbf{a}(t) = \{ a_1(t), a_2(t), \dots, a_J(t) \}$$

**临时奖励:**
$$r_j^{temp}(t) = \frac{1}{J} \cdot \left( \frac{1}{t} \eta_1 \varsigma_1 \mathcal{T}^{t} - \eta_2 \varsigma_2 \sum_{j \in \mathcal{J}} Cost_j(t) \right)$$

**最终奖励:**
$$r_j(t) = \begin{cases} r_j^{temp}(t), & \text{if all constraints are fulfilled} \\ 0, & \text{otherwise} \end{cases}$$

**动作反归一化:**
$$\ddot{a} = a(a^{max} - a^{min}) + a^{min}$$

## 13. Joint Optimization of Sensing, Communication, and Computing for Collaborative Multi-UAV Edge Computing System

### 13.1 感知模型

**成功感知概率:**
$$p_{i,k} = e^{-\varsigma R_{i,k}}$$

**感知时间:**
$$\tau_{i,k}^{sen}[t] = \tau_0 \omega_k[t]$$

**感知数据量:**
$$Q_{i,k}[t] = r_i^{sen} \tau_0 \omega_k[t]$$

**多次尝试成功概率:**
$$Pr_{i,k}[t] = 1 - (1 - p_{i,k})^{\omega_k[t]}$$

**感知满意度约束:**
$$Pr_{i,k}[t] \ge \Upsilon^{prob},\quad \alpha^{sen} \ln (1 + Q_{i,k}[t]) \ge \Upsilon^{sat}$$

### 13.2 通信模型 (NOMA D2U)

**LoS/NLoS 路径损耗:**
$$\Gamma_{u,i,k}^{LoS}[t] = \varrho_{LoS} \left( \frac{4\pi d_{u,i,k}[t]}{\lambda_0} \right),\quad \Gamma_{u,i,k}^{NLoS}[t] = \varrho_{NLoS} \left( \frac{4\pi d_{u,i,k}[t]}{\lambda_0} \right)$$

**LoS 概率:**
$$P_{u,i,k}^{LoS}[t] = \frac{1}{1 + \alpha_1 \exp(-\alpha_2 [\theta_{u,i,k}[t] - \alpha_1])}$$

其中 $\theta_{u,i,k}[t] = \frac{\pi}{180} \sin^{-1} \left( \frac{H_u}{d_{u,i,k}[t]} \right)$

**NLoS 概率:**
$$P_{u,i,k}^{NLoS}[t] = 1 - P_{u,i,k}^{LoS}[t]$$

**平均路径损耗:**
$$\Gamma_{u,i,k}[t] = \Gamma_{u,i,k}^{LoS}[t] \times P_{u,i,k}^{LoS}[t] + \Gamma_{u,i,k}^{NLoS}[t] \times P_{u,i,k}^{NLoS}[t]$$

**信道增益:**
$$g_{u,i,k}[t] = \frac{1}{\Gamma_{u,i,k}[t]}$$

**SIC 解码优先级 (信道增益降序):**
$$g_{x_{u1}[t]} > g_{x_{u2}[t]} > \cdots > g_{x_{uN}[t]}$$

等价于距离升序: $$d_{x_{u1}[t]} < d_{x_{u2}[t]} < \cdots < d_{x_{uN}[t]}$$

**UAV 接收叠加信号:**
$$\xi_u[t] = \sum_{i=1}^{N} \sqrt{p_{x_{u,i}[t]}[t] g_{x_{u,i}[t]}[t]} \zeta_{x_{u,i}}[t] + \sigma^2$$

**SINR (第i个SD):**
$$\gamma_{x_{u,i}[t]}[t] = \frac{p_{x_{u,i}[t]}[t] |g_{x_{u,i}[t]}[t]|}{\sum_{i'=i+1}^{N} p_{x_{u,i'}[t]}[t] |g_{x_{u,i'}}[t]| + \sigma^2}$$

**最后一个SD SINR:**
$$\gamma_{x_{u,N}[t]}[t] = \frac{p_{x_{u,N}[t]}[t] |g_{x_{u,N}[t]}[t]|}{\sigma^2}$$

**可达速率:**
$$r_{x_{u,i}[t]}[t] = \begin{cases} B_u \log_2(1 + \gamma_{x_{u,i}[t]}[t]), & i \ne N \\ B_u \log_2(1 + \gamma_{x_{u,N}[t]}[t]), & i = N \end{cases}$$

**NOMA传输时间 (取最大):**
$$\tau_{u,k}^{NOMA}[t] = \max \left\{ \frac{Q_{i,k}[t]}{r_{x_{u,i}[t]}[t]} \mid i \in k \right\}$$

**SD传输能耗:**
$$E_{i,k}[t] = p_{u,i,k}[t] \frac{Q_{i,k}[t]}{r_{x_{u,i}[t]}[t]}$$

### 13.3 通信模型 (OMA U2H)

**U2H信道增益:**
$$g_{u,h}[t] = \frac{1}{\Gamma_{u,h}^{LoS}[t]},\quad \Gamma_{u,h}^{LoS}[t] = \varrho_{LoS} \left( \frac{4\pi d_{u,h}[t]}{\lambda_0} \right)$$

**OFDMA子载波速率:**
$$r_{u,s}[t] = y_{u,s}[t] f_s \log_2 \left( 1 + \frac{p_u[t] g_{u,h}[t]}{f_s N_0} \right)$$

**子载波分配约束:**
$$\sum_{u \in \mathcal{U}} y_{u,s}[t] \le 1,\quad 0 < \sum_{s \in \mathcal{S}} y_{u,s}[t] \le S$$

**UAV总速率:**
$$r_u[t] = f_s \log_2 \left( 1 + \frac{p_{u,s}[t] g_{u,h}[t]}{f_s N_0} \right) \sum_{s \in \mathcal{S}} y_{u,s}[t]$$

### 13.4 计算模型

**UAV本地计算时间:**
$$\tau_{u,k}^{com}[t] = \frac{z_{u,k}[t] c_{u,k} Q_{u,k}[t]}{f_u}$$

**UAV本地计算能耗:**
$$E_u^{com}[t] = \Omega_u c_{i,k} z_{u,k}[t] Q_{u,k}[t] (f_u)^2$$

**UAV-HAP传输时间:**
$$\tau_{u,h}^{tr}[t] = \frac{(1 - z_{u,k}[t]) Q_{u,k}[t]}{r_u[t]}$$

**传输能耗:**
$$E_{u,k}^{comm}[t] = p_u^{tr}[t] \tau_{u,h}^{tr}[t]$$

**HAP计算时间:**
$$\tau_{u,k,h}^{com}[t] = \frac{(1 - z_{u,k}[t]) c_{u,k} Q_{u,k}[t]}{f_h}$$

**HAP等待时间 (FCFS):**
$$\tau_{u,k,h}^{wait}[t] = \begin{cases} 0, & \text{if } q_{u,h} = 1 \\ \tau_{u-1,k,h}[t] - (\tau_u^f[t] + \tau_{i,k}^{sen}[t] + \tau_{u,k}^{NOMA}[t] + \tau_{u,h}^{tr}[t]), & \text{if } q_{u,h} > 1 \end{cases}$$

**AEC计算时间 (取max):**
$$\tau_{u,k,h}^{AEC}[t] = \max \{ (\tau_{u,h}^{tr}[t] + \tau_{u,k,h}^{wait}[t] + \tau_{u,h}^{com}[t]), \tau_{u,k}^{com}[t] \}$$

### 13.5 控制模型

**飞行时间:**
$$\tau_u^f[t] = \frac{\|l_k - l_{k'}\|}{\bar{v}}$$

**飞行能耗:**
$$E_u^f[t] = p_u^f \frac{\|l_k - l_{k'}\|}{\bar{v}}$$

### 13.6 问题表述 (P0)

**总任务完成时间:**
$$\tau_{u,k,h}[t] = \tau_u^f[t] + \tau_{i,k}^{sen}[t] + \tau_{u,k}^{NOMA}[t] + \tau_{u,k,h}^{AEC}[t]$$

$$\min_{l_u, \boldsymbol{\omega}, \mathbf{p}_{sd}, \mathbf{p}_u, \mathbf{Y}, \mathbf{z}} \frac{\sum_{t \in \mathcal{T}} \sum_{u \in \mathcal{U}} \tau_{u,k,h}[t]}{UT}$$

$$\begin{aligned} \mathbf{P0} : \min_{l_u, \boldsymbol{\omega}, \mathbf{p}_{sd}, \mathbf{p}_u, \mathbf{Y}, \mathbf{z}} & \frac{\sum_{t \in \mathcal{T}} \sum_{u \in \mathcal{U}} \tau_{u,k,h}[t]}{UT} \\ \text{s.t. } & \text{C1} : l_u[t+1] - l_u[t] \le d^{max}, \forall t \in \mathcal{T}, \\ & \text{C2} : p_{u,i,k}[t] \le p_i^{max}, \forall u \in \mathcal{U}, i \in \mathcal{I}, k \in \mathcal{K}, \\ & \text{C3} : p_u[t] \le p_u^{max}, \forall u \in \mathcal{U}, \\ & \text{C4} : 0 \le z_{u,k}[t] \le 1, \forall u \in \mathcal{U}, k \in \mathcal{K}, \\ & \text{C5} : E_{i,k}[t] \le E_{SD}, \forall i \in \mathcal{I}, \\ & \text{C6} : E_u[t] \le E_u^{max}, \forall u \in \mathcal{U}, \\ & \text{C7} : (5), (6), (27), (28), (30). \end{aligned}$$

## 14. MADDPG-Based Resource Allocation for Integrated Communication, Sensing and Computing

### 14.1 任务模型

**任务元组:**
$$\wp_h = \{D_h, \omega_h, O_h, DI_h, T_h^{\max}\}$$

**任务创建:**
$$b_{v,h}(t) = \begin{cases} 1, & \text{车辆 v 产生了任务 h} \\ 0, & \text{otherwise} \end{cases}$$

**卸载决策:**
$$c_v(t) = \begin{cases} 1, & \text{任务卸载至无人机} \\ 0, & \text{本地处理} \end{cases}$$

**接入控制:**
$$a_{u,v}(t) = \begin{cases} 1, & \text{车辆 v 将任务卸载至无人机 u} \\ 0, & \text{otherwise} \end{cases}$$

### 14.2 通信模型

**LoS概率:**
$$p_{u,v}^{LoS}(t) = \frac{1}{1 + \alpha_1 e^{\{-\alpha_2 [\arctan(z_u(t)/x_{u,v}(t)) - \alpha_1]\}}}$$

**路径损耗:**
$$PL_{u,v}(t) = 20 \log \left( \frac{4\pi f_c \sqrt{x_{u,v}^2(t) + z_u^2(t)}}{c} \right) + p_{u,v}^{LoS}(t) \eta_{u,v}^{LoS}(t) + (1 - p_{u,v}^{LoS}(t)) \eta_{u,v}^{NLoS}(t)$$

**NOMA传输速率:**
$$R_{u,v}(t) = B_u \log \left( 1 + \frac{a_{u,v}(t) p_{u,v}(t) h_{u,v}(t)}{\sum_{v' \in \mathcal{V}} a_{u,v'}(t) p_{u,v'}(t) h_{u,v'}(t) + \sigma^2} \right)$$

### 14.3 任务处理延迟

**本地处理延迟:**
$$T_v^{loc}(t) = \frac{\sum_{h \in \mathcal{H}} b_{v,h}(t) \cdot D_h \cdot \omega_h}{f_v^{loc}}$$

**卸载总延迟:**
$$T_v^{off}(t) = \sum_{u \in \mathcal{U}} \sum_{h \in \mathcal{H}} \left[ a_{u,v,h}^{idle}(t) \left( \frac{b_{v,h}(t) D_h}{R_{u,v}(t)} + \frac{b_{v,h}(t) D_h \omega_h}{f_{u,v}(t)} \right) + a_{u,v,h}^{new}(t) \left( T_{u,h}^{place} + \frac{b_{v,h}(t) D_h}{R_{u,v}(t)} + \frac{b_{v,h}(t) D_h \omega_h}{f_{u,v}(t)} \right) \right]$$

**总延迟:**
$$T_{all}(t) = (1 - c_v(t)) T_v^{loc}(t) + c_v(t) T_v^{off}(t)$$

### 14.4 经济支出

**本地经济支出:**
$$Exp_v^{loc}(t) = \eta_v^{loc} f_v^{loc}$$

**卸载经济支出:**
$$Exp_v^{off}(t) = \sum_{u \in \mathcal{U}} \sum_{h \in \mathcal{H}} [a_{u,v,h}^{idle}(t)(\eta_u^{com} R_{u,v}(t) + \eta_u^{comp} f_{u,v}(t)) + a_{u,v,h}^{new}(t)(\eta_u^{new} + \eta_u^{com} R_{u,v}(t) + \eta_u^{comp} f_{u,v}(t))]$$

**总支出:**
$$Exp_{all}(t) = (1 - c_v(t)) Exp_v^{loc}(t) + c_v(t) Exp_v^{off}(t)$$

### 14.5 感知模型 (雷达 MI)

**MI:**
$$MI_v(t) = \frac{1}{2} S T_S B_u \log_2 (1 + \Gamma_v(t))$$

**雷达 SINR:**
$$\Gamma_v(t) = \frac{a_{u,v}(t) p_{u,v}(t) S T_S^2 |Q_v(f_c)|^2}{\sum_{v' \in \mathcal{V}} a_{u,v'}(t) p_{u,v'}(t) h_{v,v'}(t) + \sigma^2}$$

**目标响应函数:**
$$Q_v(f_c) = \int_{-\infty}^{+\infty} q_v(t) e^{-j 2 \pi f_c t} dt$$

其中 $q_v(t) = G \cdot RCS \cdot \xi$

**总MI:**
$$MI_{all}(t) = \sum_{v \in \mathcal{V}} MI_v(t)$$

### 14.6 Cobb-Douglas 效用函数

$$F_u(t) = \frac{MI_{all}^\alpha(\Lambda, \Pi)}{Exp_{all}^\beta(\Lambda, \Theta, \Pi, \Xi) \cdot T_{all}^\gamma(\Lambda, \Theta, \Pi, \Xi)}$$

其中 $\alpha + \beta + \gamma = 1$

### 14.7 问题表述

$$\max_{\Lambda, \Theta, \Pi, \Xi} \sum_{u \in \mathcal{U}} F_u(\Lambda, \Theta, \Pi, \Xi)$$

$$\begin{aligned} \max_{\Lambda, \Theta, \Pi, \Xi} &\ \sum_{u \in \mathcal{U}} F_u(\Lambda, \Theta, \Pi, \Xi) \\ \text{s.t.} \\ C1&: a_{u,v}(t), a_{u,v,h}^{\text{idle}}, a_{u,v,h}^{\text{new}}, b_{v,h}(t), c_v(t) \in \{0,1\}, \\ C2&: \sum_{u \in \mathcal{U}} a_{u,v}(t) \cdot \mathbb{I}\{c_v(t) = 1\} = 1, \\ C3&: \sum_{u \in \mathcal{U}} a_{u,v}(t) c_v(t) = 1, \\ C4&: a_{u,v,h}^{\text{idle}}(t) + a_{u,v,h}^{\text{new}}(t) = a_{u,v}(t) b_{v,h}(t), \\ C5&: \sum_{h \in \mathcal{H}} n_{h,u}(t) \cdot D I_h \leq C_u, \\ C6&: \sum_{v \in \mathcal{V}} a_{u,v}(t) b_{v,h}(t) c_v(t) \leq n_{h,u}(t), \\ C7&: MI_v(t) \geq MI_v^{\min}, \\ C8&: 0 < p_{u,v}(t) < p_{\max}, \\ C9&: 0 < f_{u,v}(t) < f_{\max}, \\ C10&: \sum_{v \in \mathcal{V}} f_{u,v}(t) \leq f_{\max}. \end{aligned}$$

### 14.8 MADDPG 模型

**局部观测:**
$$o_u(t) \triangleq \{b_{v,h}(t); n_{h,u}(t-1); \varepsilon_{u,v}(t); x_{u,v}(t); z_u(t)\}$$

**全局状态:**
$$s(t) = \{o_1(t), \dots, o_U(t)\}$$

**动作:**
$$a_u(t) \triangleq \{a_{u,v}(t), a_{u,v,h}^{idle}, a_{u,v,h}^{new}; c_v(t); p_{u,v}(t); f_{u,v}(t)\}$$

**全局动作:**
$$\mathbf{a}(t) = \{a_1(t), \dots, a_U(t)\}$$

**奖励:**
$$r_u(t) = \begin{cases} \frac{\sum_{u \in \mathcal{U}} F_u(t)}{U}, & \text{如果所有约束均满足} \\ 0, & \text{否则} \end{cases}$$

## 15. Minimizing maximum latency of task offloading for multi-UAV-assisted maritime search and rescue

### 15.1 感知模型

**HFOV/VFOV:**
$$H_{\text{FOV}}(k) = 2H_n(k) \tan \left( \frac{\phi_h}{2} \right)$$
$$V_{\text{FOV}}(k) = 2H_n(k) \tan \left( \frac{\phi_v}{2} \right)$$

**S-UAV覆盖区域:**
$$L_n(k) \times W_n(k) = [x_n(k) - H_n(k) \tan(\phi_h/2), x_n(k) + H_n(k) \tan(\phi_h/2)] \times [y_n(k) - H_n(k) \tan(\phi_v/2), y_n(k) + H_n(k) \tan(\phi_v/2)]$$

**目标监测变量:**
$$\alpha_{i,n}(k) = \begin{cases} 1 & \text{目标 i 由 S-UAV n 监测} \\ 0 & \text{otherwise} \end{cases}$$

**覆盖约束:**
$$\sum_{n \in \mathcal{N}} \alpha_{i,n}(k) \ge 1$$

### 15.2 控制模型

**S-UAV水平坐标 (取中点):**
$$x_n(k) = \frac{\max_{i \in \mathcal{I}(n)} \{x_i(k)\} + \min_{i \in \mathcal{I}(n)} \{x_i(k)\}}{2}$$
$$y_n(k) = \frac{\max_{i \in \mathcal{I}(n)} \{y_i(k)\} + \min_{i \in \mathcal{I}(n)} \{y_i(k)\}}{2}$$

**S-UAV高度:**
$$H_n(k) = \max \left\{ \frac{\max_{i \in \mathcal{I}(n)} \{x_i(k)\} - \min_{i \in \mathcal{I}(n)} \{x_i(k)\}}{2 \tan(\phi_h/2)}, \frac{\max_{i \in \mathcal{I}(n)} \{y_i(k)\} - \min_{i \in \mathcal{I}(n)} \{y_i(k)\}}{2 \tan(\phi_v/2)} \right\} + \gamma$$

### 15.3 通信模型

**信道增益 (自由空间):**
$$g_n(k) = \rho_0 d_n^{-2}(k) = \frac{\rho_0}{\|\mathbf{q}_n(k) - \mathbf{q}_M(k)\|^2}$$

**传输速率:**
$$R_n(k) = B \log_2 \left( 1 + \frac{p_n(k)g_n(k)}{\sigma^2} \right) = B \log_2 \left( 1 + \frac{\Gamma_1}{\|\mathbf{q}_n(k) - \mathbf{q}_M(k)\|^2} \right)$$

其中 $\Gamma_1 = \frac{\rho_0 p_n(k)}{\sigma^2}$

### 15.4 计算模型

**卸载指示:**
$$\beta_n(k) = \begin{cases} 1 & \text{S-UAV n 将任务卸载到 R-UAV} \\ 0 & \text{本地处理} \end{cases}$$

**本地计算时间:**
$$t_n^{\text{loc}} = \frac{S_n(k)f_0}{f_n(k)}$$

**本地处理后传输时延:**
$$t_{n,M}^{\text{loc}} = \frac{\mu_n S_n(k)}{B \log_2 \left( 1 + \frac{\Gamma_1}{\|\mathbf{q}_n(k) - \mathbf{q}_M(k)\|^2} \right)}$$

**卸载传输时延:**
$$t_{n,M}^{\text{off}} = \frac{S_n(k)}{B \log_2 \left( 1 + \frac{\Gamma_1}{\|\mathbf{q}_n(k) - \mathbf{q}_M(k)\|^2} \right)}$$

**R-UAV计算时间:**
$$t_M^{\text{off}} = \frac{S_n(k)f_0}{f_M(k) / \sum_{n \in \mathcal{N}} \beta_n(k)}$$

**S-UAV总时延:**
$$T_n^{\text{total}} = (1 - \beta_n(k))(t_n^{\text{loc}} + t_{n,M}^{\text{loc}}) + \beta_n(k)(t_{n,M}^{\text{off}} + t_M^{\text{off}})$$

### 15.5 能耗模型

**S-UAV通信能耗:**
$$E_n^{\text{com}}(k) = p_n(k) [(1 - \beta_n(k))t_{n,M}^{\text{loc}} + \beta_n(k)t_{n,M}^{\text{off}}]$$

**S-UAV计算能耗:**
$$E_n^{\text{comp}}(k) = (1 - \beta_n(k)) f_n^2(k) \zeta S_n(k) f_0$$

**S-UAV总能耗:**
$$E_n(k) = E_n^{\text{com}}(k) + E_n^{\text{comp}}(k) + E_n^{\text{hover}}(k)$$

**R-UAV计算能耗:**
$$E_{M,n}^{\text{comp}}(k) = \sum_{n \in \mathcal{N}} \beta_n(k) f_M^2(k) \zeta f_0 S_n(k)$$

**R-UAV总能耗:**
$$E_M(k) = \sum_{n \in \mathcal{N}} \beta_n(k) E_{M,n}^{\text{comp}}(k) + E_M^{\text{hover}}(k)$$

### 15.6 问题表述 (P1)

其中 $U_{LOS} = 3\text{dB}, U_{NLOS} = 23\text{dB}$

$$P_{\text{LoS}}(\theta) = \frac{1}{1 + a \exp(-b(\theta^\circ - a))}$$

$$L = P_{\text{LoS}} L_{\text{LoS}} + P_{\text{NLoS}} L_{\text{NLoS}}$$

**信道增益:**
$$g = 10^{-\bar L / 10}$$

**带宽分配:**
$$B_k = \frac{B}{K}$$

**接收功率:**
$$P_r = \frac{P_T}{K} \cdot g$$

**SNR:**
$$SNR = \frac{P_r}{N_0 B}$$

**Shannon速率:**
$$R = \frac{B}{K} \log_2(1 + SNR)$$

### 17.3 覆盖模型

**覆盖判据:**
$$R_{i,m}(z_m, r_{i,m}, K_m) \ge R_{th}$$

### 17.4 因果链

$$\mathbf{a}_m^t = (\Delta x_m,\; \Delta y_m,\; \Delta z_m)$$

离散化: 0=悬停, 1-4=水平移动, 5-6=上升/下降

$$r_i^t = \alpha \cdot \text{CoverageGain} - \beta \cdot \text{EnergyCost} - \gamma \cdot \text{OverlapPenalty} - \delta \cdot \text{ViolationPenalty}$$

## 18. Task assignment and exploration optimization for low altitude UAV rescue via generative AI enhanced multi-agent reinforcement learning

### 18.1 控制模型

**飞行距离:**
$$d_u(t_i) = \|L_u(t_i) - L_u(t_{i-1})\| = \sqrt{(x_u(t_i) - x_u(t_{i-1}))^2 + (y_u(t_i) - y_u(t_{i-1}))^2}$$

**UAV状态:**
$$s_u(t_i) = (L_u(t_i), \theta_u(t_i), v_u(t_i))$$

### 18.2 通信模型

**Nakagami-m衰落 (LoS):**
$$H_{u,j}^L(t_i) = \frac{2(w_L)^{w_L} h^{(2w_L-1)} e^{-\frac{w_L h^2}{p}}}{\Gamma(w_L) \sqrt{p^{w_L}}}$$

**Nakagami-m衰落 (NLoS):**
$$H_{u,j}^{NL}(t_i) = \frac{2(w_{NL})^{w_{NL}} h^{(2w_{NL}-1)} e^{-\frac{w_{NL} h^2}{p}}}{\Gamma(w_{NL}) \sqrt{p^{w_{NL}}}}$$

**LoS/NLoS路径损耗:**
$$L_{u,j}^L(t_i) = \frac{(4\pi d_0 f_c)^2}{c^2} \left(\frac{d_{u,j}(t_i)}{d_0}\right)^{\beta_L},\quad L_{u,j}^{NL}(t_i) = \frac{(4\pi d_0 f_c)^2}{c^2} \left(\frac{d_{u,j}(t_i)}{d_0}\right)^{\beta_{NL}}$$

**阴影衰落:**
$$F_{\sigma}^L(t_i) \sim \mathcal{N}(0, (\sigma_L)^2),\quad F_{\sigma}^{NL}(t_i) \sim \mathcal{N}(0, (\sigma_{NL})^2)$$

**LoS/NLoS增益:**
$$l_{u,j}^L(t_i) = |H_{u,j}^L(t_i)|^2 [L_{u,j}^L(t_i)]^{-1} 10^{-\frac{F_{\sigma}^L(t_i)}{10}}$$
$$l_{u,j}^{NL}(t_i) = |H_{u,j}^{NL}(t_i)|^2 [L_{u,j}^{NL}(t_i)]^{-1} 10^{-\frac{F_{\sigma}^{NL}(t_i)}{10}}$$

**信道功率增益:**
$$l_{u,j}(t_i) = p_{u,j}^L l_{u,j}^L(t_i) + (1 - p_{u,j}^L) l_{u,j}^{NL}(t_i)$$

**数据传输速率:**
$$R_{u,j}(t_i) = B_w \log_2 \left(1 + \frac{P_u l_{u,j}(t_i)}{\sigma^2}\right)$$

### 18.3 计算模型

**UAV本地计算延迟:**
$$T_u^{\text{UAV}}(t_i) = \frac{\varsigma_{u,j}(t_i) D_u(t_i) C_u}{f_u}$$

**传输延迟:**
$$T_{tran}^{u,j}(t_i) = \frac{(1 - \varsigma_{u,j}(t_i)) D_u(t_i)}{R_{u,j}(t_i)}$$

**GER处理延迟:**
$$T_{comp}^j(t_i) = \frac{(1 - \varsigma_{u,j}(t_i)) D_u(t_i) C_u}{f_{j,u}(t_i)}$$

**GER总服务延迟:**
$$T_{GER}^{u,j}(t_i) = T_{tran}^{u,j}(t_i) + T_{comp}^j(t_i)$$

**总任务完成延迟:**
$$T_{tota}(t_i) = \sum_{u=1}^U \sum_{j=1}^J (T_{UAV}^u(t_i) + T_{GER}^{u,j}(t_i))$$

### 18.4 能耗模型

**传输能耗:**
$$E_{tran}^{u,j}(t_i) = P_u T_{tran}^{u,j}(t_i)$$

**计算能耗:**
$$E_{comp}^u(t_i) = \kappa_u (f_u)^2 D_u(t_i) C_u$$

**推进能耗:**
$$E_{prop}^u(t_i) = w_u v_u(t_i)^2$$

**探测能耗:**
$$E_z^u(t_i) = \varpi \sum_{k=1}^m d_{zk},\quad E_{dete}^u(t_i) = \sum_{z \in Z} E_z^u(t_i)$$

**总能耗:**
$$E_{tota}(t_i) = E_{tran}^{u,j}(t_i) + E_{comp}^u(t_i) + E_{prop}^u(t_i) + E_{dete}^u(t_i)$$

**长期平均约束:**
$$\frac{1}{T} \sum_{i=1}^I \mathbb{E}[E_{tota}(t_i)] \leq \bar{E}_u$$

### 18.5 问题表述 (P1)

$$\min_{\mathbf{F}, \mathbf{M}, \mathbf{W}} \sum_{i=1}^{I} T^{tota}(t_i)$$

$$\begin{aligned} \mathbf{P1} : \min_{\mathbf{F}, \mathbf{M}, \mathbf{W}} \quad & \left\{ \sum_{i=1}^{I} T^{tota}(t_i) \right\} \\ \text{s.t.} \quad & \frac{1}{T} \sum_{i=1}^{I} \mathbb{E}[E^{tota}(t_i)] \leq \bar{E}_u, \forall u \in \mathcal{U}, & (20\text{a}) \\ & 0 \leq \varsigma_{u,j}(t_i) \leq 1, \forall u \in \mathcal{U}, j \in \mathcal{J}, & (20\text{b}) \\ & \sum_{j=1}^{J} \varsigma_{u,j}(t_i) \leq 1, \forall u \in \mathcal{U}, & (20\text{c}) \\ & \sum_{u=1}^{U} \varsigma_{u,j}(t_i) \leq 1, \forall j \in \mathcal{J}, & (20\text{d}) \\ & \varsigma_{u,j}(t_i) \cdot T^{UAV}(t_i) \leq \tau_u, \forall u \in \mathcal{U}, j \in \mathcal{J}, & (20\text{e}) \\ & \varsigma_{u,j}(t_i) \cdot T^{GER}(t_i) \leq \tau_u, \forall u \in \mathcal{U}, j \in \mathcal{J}, & (20\text{f}) \\ & 0 \leq f_{j,u}(t_i) \leq f_j^{\max}, \forall u \in \mathcal{U}, j \in \mathcal{J}, & (20\text{g}) \\ & \sum_{u=1}^{U} f_{j,u}(t_i) \leq f_j^{\max}, \forall j \in \mathcal{J}, & (20\text{h}) \\ & \delta \cdot d_u^{\min} / T \leq d_u(t_i) \leq \delta \cdot V_u^{\max}, \forall j \in \mathcal{J}, & (20\text{i}) \\ & \sum_{i=1}^{I} d_u(t_i) \leq L_u^{\max}, \forall u \in \mathcal{U}, j \in \mathcal{J}, & (20\text{j}) \\ & \|L_u(t_i) - L_{u+1}(t_i)\| \geq D, \forall u \in \mathcal{U}, j \in \mathcal{J}. & (20\text{k}) \end{aligned}$$

**求解方法**: 外层 Lyapunov 优化 + 内层 HG-MADDPG (含 GDM 扩散模型 Actor + 匈牙利算法探索区域选择)

## 19. UAV-assisted edge inference with integrated sensing, communication, and computation

### 19.1 无人机轨迹模型

**连续飞行能耗 (理论公式):**
$$\hat{E}_{\text{traj}} = \int_{0}^{T} \left[ c_1(v(t))^3 + \frac{c_2}{v(t)} \left( 1 + \frac{(a(t))^2 - \frac{(a(t)v(t))^2}{(v(t))^2}}{g^2} \right) \right] dt + \frac{1}{2}m_{uav}((v(T))^2 - (v(0))^2)$$

**飞行段持续时间:**
$$T_i = \|\mathbf{p}[i + 1] - \mathbf{p}[i]\|_2 / v_i$$

**单段飞行能耗 (匀速):**
$$E_{\text{traj}}[i] = T_i \left( c_1 v_i^3 + \frac{c_2}{v_i} \right)$$

**总飞行能耗:**
$$E_{\text{traj}} = \sum_{i=0}^K T_i \left( c_1 v_i^3 + \frac{c_2}{v_i} \right) + (K + 2)\tilde{E}_{\text{traj}}$$

### 19.2 感知与特征提取模型

**含噪感知数据:**
$$\mathbf{x}_k = \mathbf{x}_k^{(0)} + \hat{\mathbf{n}}_k$$

**感知噪声分布:**
$$\hat{\mathbf{n}}_k \sim \mathcal{N} \left( \mathbf{0}, \frac{\delta_k^2}{P_{0,k}} \mathbf{I} \right)$$

**理想特征向量:**
$$\mathbf{z}_k^{(0)} = F(\mathbf{x}_k^{(0)})$$

**GMM 特征分布:**
$$f(\mathbf{z}_k^{(0)}[m]) = \frac{1}{L} \sum_{\ell=1}^L \mathcal{N}(\mu_{\ell,k,m}, \sigma_{k,m}^2)$$

**一阶泰勒线性化:**
$$F(\mathbf{x}) \approx F(\mathbf{x}_0) + \nabla F(\mathbf{x}_0)^\mathsf{T} (\mathbf{x} - \mathbf{x}_0)$$

**含噪特征分量:**
$$\mathbf{z}_k[m] = F(\mathbf{x}_k^{(0)} + \hat{\mathbf{n}}_k)[m] \approx F(\mathbf{x}_k^{(0)})[m] + \nabla F(\mathbf{x}_k^{(0)})^\mathsf{T}[m] \cdot \hat{\mathbf{n}}_k$$

**噪声增强的GMM分布:**
$$f(\mathbf{z}_k[m]) = \frac{1}{L} \sum_{\ell=1}^L \mathcal{N} \left( \mu_{\ell,k,m}, \sigma_{k,m}^2 + \sum_{i=1}^{\hat{\mathcal{M}}} \lambda_k[i, m]^2 \frac{\delta_k^2}{P_{0,k}} \right)$$

### 19.3 能量与延迟模型

**感知能耗:**
$$E_{0,i,k} = P_{0,i,k}\hat{T}_0$$

**特征提取时延:**
$$\hat{T}_{1,i,k} = L_k/f_k$$

**特征提取能耗:**
$$E_{1,i,k} = \beta f_k^2 L_k$$

### 19.4 通信模型

**设备-UAV SNR:**
$$\text{SNR}_{i,k} = h_{i,k}P_2/\sigma^2$$

**信道增益:**
$$h_{i,k} = \frac{\gamma_{i,k}\beta_0}{H^2 + \|\mathbf{p}[i] - \mathbf{q}[k]\|_2^2}$$

**可达速率:**
$$R_{i,k} = B \log_2(1 + \text{SNR}_{i,k})$$

**量化/反量化:**
$$\hat{\mathbf{z}}_k = \mathcal{Q}(\mathbf{z}_k),\quad \tilde{\mathbf{z}}_k = \mathcal{DQ}(\hat{\mathbf{z}}_k) = \mathbf{z}_k + e$$

其中 $e \sim \mathcal{N}(0, a^2/(3Q^2))$

**通信容量约束:**
$$R_{i,k}\hat{T}_{2,i,k} \geq L_\text{H} = M \log_2 Q$$

**UAV-服务器SNR:**
$$\text{SNR} = h\tilde{P}_3/\sigma^2$$

其中 $h = \frac{\beta_0}{H^2 + \|\mathbf{p}[K + 1] - \mathbf{q}[K + 1]\|_2^2}$

**通信能耗:**
$$E_{2,i,k} = P_{2,i,k}\hat{T}_{2,i,k},\quad \tilde{E}_{2,k} = \sum_{i=0}^{K} \gamma_{i,k} \tilde{P}_2 \hat{T}_{2,i,k}$$

**UAV卸载能耗:**
$$\tilde{E}_3 = \tilde{P}_3 \tilde{T}_3$$

**悬停时间/能耗:**
$$T_{h,i,k} = \gamma_{i,k}(\hat{T}_0 + \hat{T}_{1,k} + \hat{T}_{2,i,k}),\quad E_{h,i,k} = P_h T_{h,i,k}$$

**总悬停能耗:**
$$E_h = P_h \left( \sum_{k=1}^K \sum_{i=0}^K \gamma_{i,k}(\hat{T}_0 + \hat{T}_{1,k} + \hat{T}_{2,i,k}) + \tilde{T}_3 \right)$$

### 19.5 推理准确度指标 (判别增益)

**单维判别增益:**
$$G(\tilde{\mathbf{z}}_k[m]; \ell, \ell') = \text{D}_{KL}[f_\ell(\tilde{\mathbf{z}}_k[m]) \| f_{\ell'}(\tilde{\mathbf{z}}_k[m])] + \text{D}_{KL}[f_{\ell'}(\tilde{\mathbf{z}}_k[m]) \| f_\ell(\tilde{\mathbf{z}}_k[m])]$$

**含噪+量化判别增益:**
$$G(\tilde{\mathbf{z}}_k[m]; \ell, \ell') = \frac{(\mu_{\ell,k,m} - \mu_{\ell',k,m})^2}{\sigma_{k,m}^2 + \sum_{m=1}^{\hat{M}} (\lambda_k[m])^2 \delta_k^2/P_{0,k} + \sigma_q^2}$$

**总判别增益:**
$$G(\tilde{\mathbf{z}}_k; \ell, \ell') = \sum_{m=1}^M \frac{(\mu_{\ell,k,m} - \mu_{\ell',k,m})^2}{\sigma_{k,m}^2 + \sum_{m=1}^{\hat{M}} (\lambda_k[m])^2 \delta_k^2/P_{0,k} + \sigma_q^2}$$

### 19.6 问题表述 (P1)

**目标函数:**
$$\min \left\{ \sum_{i=0}^{K} T_i + \sum_{k=1}^K \sum_{i=0}^K \gamma_{i,k}(\hat{T}_0 + \hat{T}_{1,i,k} + \hat{T}_{2,i,k}) + \tilde{T}_3 + T_4 \right\}$$

$$\begin{aligned} \text{(P1)} \quad \min_{\{\gamma_{i,k}\}, \{v_i\}, \{p[i]\}, \{f_k\}, \{P_{0,k}\}, \{P_{2,i,k}\}, \tilde{P}_3, \{\hat{T}_{2,k}\}, \tilde{T}_3} & \left\{ \sum_{i=0}^{K} T_i + \sum_{k=1}^K \sum_{i=0}^K \gamma_{i,k}(\hat{T}_0 + \hat{T}_{1,i,k} + \hat{T}_{2,i,k}) + \tilde{T}_3 + T_4 \right\} \\ \text{s.t.} \quad & \text{(C1) } \mathbf{p}[0], \mathbf{q}[k], \mathbf{q}[K+1] \text{ 位置固定} \\ & \text{(C2) } \gamma_{i,k} \in \{0,1\}, \sum_{k} \gamma_{i,k} = 1, \sum_{i} \gamma_{i,k} = 1 \\ & \text{(C3) } v_i \leq V_{max} \\ & \text{(C4) } E_{\text{traj}} + E_h \leq \tilde{\mathcal{E}} \\ & \text{(C5) } \sum_{i=0}^K \gamma_{i,k}(E_{0,i,k} + E_{1,i,k} + E_{2,i,k}) \leq \mathcal{E}_k \\ & \text{(C6) } G(\tilde{\mathbf{z}}_k; \ell, \ell') \geq G_L \\ & \text{(C7) } \gamma_{i,k} M \log_2 Q \leq \gamma_{i,k} \hat{T}_{2,i,k} B \log_2(1 + h_{i,k} P_{2,i,k}/\sigma^2) \\ & \text{(C8) } \sum_{k} M_k \log_2 Q \leq \tilde{T}_3 B \log_2(1 + h \tilde{P}_3/\sigma^2) \\ & \text{(C9) } f_k \leq f_U \\ & \text{(C10) } P_{2,i,k} \leq P_{2_U} \\ & \text{(C11) } \tilde{P}_3 \leq P_{3_U} \end{aligned}$$

## 20. UAV_time_window_RL-master

### 20.1 UAV运动模型

**位置更新 (极坐标):**
$$\mathbf{p}_i(t+1) = \mathbf{p}_i(t) + v_i(t) \begin{bmatrix} \cos\theta_i(t) \\ \sin\theta_i(t) \end{bmatrix}$$

其中 $v_i(t) \in [0, V_{\max}], \theta_i(t) \in [-\pi, \pi]$

**混合动作空间:**
$$\mathbf{a}_i(t) = (v_i(t), \theta_i(t), k_i(t))$$

其中 $k_i(t) \in \{1, 2, \dots, M\}$ 为离散任务选择

**动作空间:**
$$\mathcal{A}_i = [0, V_{\max}] \times [-\pi, \pi] \times \{1, \dots, M\}$$

### 20.2 任务与时间窗

**任务位置:**
$$\mathbf{q}_j = (x_j, y_j)$$

**时间窗:**
$$\mathcal{W}_j = [t_j^{\text{start}}, t_j^{\text{end}}]$$

**任务成功条件:**
$$\exists i \text{ s.t. } \begin{cases} \|\mathbf{p}_i(t) - \mathbf{q}_j\| \le d_{th} \\ t \in \mathcal{W}_j \end{cases}$$

### 20.3 状态空间

**局部观测:**
$$\mathbf{o}_i(t) = \left[x_i(t), y_i(t), t, \sum_{j=1}^M \mathbb{I}_j(t)\right]$$

**全局状态 (Critic用):**
$$\mathbf{s}(t) = (\mathbf{o}_1(t), \mathbf{o}_2(t), \dots, \mathbf{o}_N(t))$$

### 20.4 奖励函数

**即时奖励:**
$$r_i(t) = r_i^{\text{task}}(t) - r_i^{\text{collision}}(t) - r_i^{\text{energy}}(t)$$

**任务完成奖励:**
$$r_i^{\text{task}}(t) = \begin{cases} R_{\text{task}}, & \text{若 UAV i 在时间窗内完成任务} \\ 0, & \text{否则} \end{cases}$$

**能耗公平性指标 (评估):**
$$\mathcal{F} = \frac{(\sum_i E_i)^2}{N \sum_i E_i^2}$$

### 20.5 优化目标

$$\max_{\pi} \mathbb{E}_\pi\left[\sum_{t=0}^T \gamma^t \sum_{i=1}^N r_i(t)\right]$$

### 20.6 MAPPO 策略

**策略参数化 (混合动作):**
$$\pi_\theta(\mathbf{a}_i|\mathbf{o}_i) = \pi_\theta(v_i|\mathbf{o}_i) \cdot \pi_\theta(\theta_i|\mathbf{o}_i) \cdot \pi_\theta(k_i|\mathbf{o}_i)$$

连续动作用高斯分布，离散任务选择用 categorical 分布

**PPO Clip目标:**
$$\mathcal{L}^{\text{CLIP}}(\theta) = \mathbb{E}\left[\min\left(r_t(\theta) A_t,\; \text{clip}(r_t(\theta), 1-\epsilon, 1+\epsilon) A_t\right)\right]$$

**Critic MSE:**
$$\mathcal{L}_V(\phi) = \mathbb{E}\left[(V_\phi(\mathbf{s}_t) - R_t)^2\right]$$

| Nakagami-m衰落 | $2w^w h^{2w-1} e^{-w h^2/p} / (\Gamma(w)\sqrt{p^w})$ | 18 |
| 香农容量 | $R = B \log_2(1 + SNR)$ | 几乎所有论文 |

### B. 计算模型 (Computation Models)

| 公式 | 模式 | 
|------|------|
| 本地计算时延 | $T_{loc} = D \cdot C / f$ |

| 传输能耗 | $E_{trans} = P_{tx} \cdot T_{trans}$ |
| 队列动态 (Lyapunov) | $Q(t+1) = \max(Q(t) + A(t) - D(t), 0)$ |
| Little定律 | $T = Q / \lambda$ |
